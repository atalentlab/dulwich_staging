Discover Our Student-Centred Learning at Dulwich International Schools

Catering to a wide-range of students, our international schools in Asia and China offer differing curriculum models but all provide personalised, student-centred learning pathways complemented by a focus on well-being and opportunities beyond the classroom through meaningful partnerships with world-class institutions.

Dulwich College International Schools: K-12 & IB/A-Level Schools in Asia

Dulwich College International Schools are K-12 schools that offer an adapted English National Curriculum, the IGCSE and then the IB Diploma Programme.

Dulwich International High School Programmes: IGCSE & A-Level Pathways

Dulwich International High School Programmes cater to students aged 14-18 and offer the IGCSE followed by the A-level examinations. 

Dehong International Chinese Schools: Bilingual & IB Programme

Dehong International Chinese Schools are schools that nurture bilingual and bicultural students and offer a combined programme comprising the Chinese compulsory curriculum enhanced with an international educational philosophy, culminating with the IB Diploma.

Please select the school you are interested in to learn more about its programmes and admissions process.
